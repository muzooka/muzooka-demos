Popular Ticketing Options

  • 3-Day General Admission

    A 3-Day General Admission ticket with campground access is non-transferable and good for one (1) person all weekend, Friday through Sunday. Allows for festival access to all concerts, vendors, activities, and campgrounds. Camping options (RV, car, or tent camping) must be purchased separately. Children 10 and under do not require an admission ticket or a camp access pass. Accessible Viewing area located Southeast of the GA Area. Single Day General Admission passes will be available at a later date in the spring.

  • VIP with Campground Access

    A 3-Day VIP pass to Mountain Jam gives you access to a special VIP viewing area in front of the main stage. A VIP pass also gives you exclusive access to the VIP Hospitality Lounge Tent with complimentary snacks, hors d'oeuvres, discounted cash bar, private restrooms, exclusive artist performances in the VIP area, entry to the VIP Campgrounds with free hot showers and 24-hour access to indoor bathrooms. You'll also have the ability to purchase camping in VIP RV and VIP Car Camping or any campground of your choice.

    *If interested in also staying at the on-site hotel, Kaatskill Mountain Lodge, please contact Mountain Jam directly.

  • Mountain Top Car Camping

    A Mountain Top Car Camping pass is located above the festival field on top of the mountain. It includes a 10x30’ campsite on grass. A Mountain Top Car Camping pass is good for one (1) steering mechanism and one (1) tent per campsite (RVs are not allowed in this area). Each individual on the campsite must have an admission ticket with campground access. All campsites are first come first serve, no vehicle re-entry allowed.
